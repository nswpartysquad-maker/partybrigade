Terms & Conditions

Important booking terms for NSW Party Brigade.

This standalone page keeps your terms on your own domain instead of linking back to another website.

Children’s party terms

  • Children’s parties are for 12 years of age and under.
  • At least 2 supervising adults must be on board for each ride duration.
  • Minimum $250 deposit applies.
  • $120 per agreed extra 15 minutes.
  • Outer suburbs surcharge $80.
  • $250 per hour thereafter for out of area travel.
  • Toll way usage $25 per usage.
  • No alcohol may be consumed by passengers under 18 years of age.
  • Passenger numbers may be reduced when booking onboard entertainment.

General safety conditions

  • Strictly no standing on seats.
  • No standing while the vehicle is in motion unless instructed by crew.
  • Please remain seated and move to entry or exit points only when instructed.
  • No hanging on ladders or handrails.
  • No arms, hands, heads or body parts outside the perimeter of the vehicle.
  • No throwing items inside or outside the vehicle.
  • Any damage, injuries or spills must be reported immediately.
  • Crew may refuse entry to intoxicated clients and no refund applies.

Operational and business notes

  • Engine Number 9 NSW PTY LTD is stated as an accredited public passenger vehicle operator.
  • All fire engines are stated as accredited, maintained and routinely inspected.
  • Drivers are stated as authorised and accredited.
  • Working With Children policy applies to staff attending children’s packages.
  • Vehicles carry first aid kits, bottled water and fire extinguishers.
