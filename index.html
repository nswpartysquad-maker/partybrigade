FAQ

Quick answers for busy parents.

Simple answers reduce hesitation and help the page convert more enquiries.

What ages is this for?

Children’s party bookings are designed for ages 0 to 12.

How many people can attend?

Up to 20 people in total, usually 18 children and 2 adults.

Is Fireman Dad only on certain packages?

No. Fireman Dad is available for all packages.

Do you need a deposit?

Yes, a deposit secures the booking date.

Can we add Fireman Dan?

Yes. Fireman Dan can be added for families wanting more interactive entertainment.

Do you travel across Sydney?

Yes, with surcharges potentially applying for some outer suburbs.
