Quick answers

Easy information for parents without losing the fun.

Which package has no ride?

The Blue Fire Truck Dance Party is the stationary only package with nil ride and up to 1 hour party time.

Which package is best for ages 2 to 4?

Your Yellow Fire Truck Party and Ride page specifically recommends Yellow for children aged 2 to 4 years.

Can larger groups split the ride?

Yes. Orange can split 30 minutes into 2 x 15 minute rides, and Red can split into 2 x 30 minute or 4 x 15 minute rides.

Can Fireman Dad go on any package?

Yes. Fireman Dad is available with Blue, Yellow, Orange and Red.

Do kids get certificates?

Yes. The package pages include official frameable assistant certificates and photo opportunities around the fire hose prop.

Can Fireman Dan be booked with or without the truck?

Yes. The Fireman Dan page says hire can be independent of fire truck package hire.

Terms & Conditions

Important booking terms for children’s parties.

This section is based on the current Engine Number 9 Terms & Conditions page for children’s parties and general hire rules.

Children’s party terms

  • Children’s parties are for 12 years of age and under.
  • At least 2 supervising adults must be on board for each ride duration.
  • Minimum $250 deposit applies.
  • $120 per agreed extra 15 minutes.
  • Outer suburbs surcharge $80.
  • $250 per hour thereafter for out of area travel.
  • Toll way usage $25 per usage.
  • No alcohol may be consumed by passengers under 18 years of age.
  • Passenger numbers may be reduced when booking onboard entertainment.

General conditions

  • Strictly no standing on seats.
  • No standing while the vehicle is in motion unless instructed by crew.
  • Please remain seated and move to entry or exit points only when instructed.
  • No hanging on ladders or handrails.
  • No arms, hands, heads or body parts outside the perimeter of the vehicle.
  • No throwing items inside or outside the vehicle.
  • Any damage, injuries or spills must be reported immediately.
  • Crew may refuse entry to intoxicated clients and no refund applies.
