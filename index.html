Things You Should Know

Safety, accreditation and the fine print — all in one place.

We take children’s transport, compliance and event safety seriously, so families know they are booking with a professional operator.

Professional & Accredited

  • Engine Number 9 NSW PTY LTD is an Accredited Public Passenger Vehicle Operator.
  • All fire engines in our fleet are accredited, routinely maintained and regularly inspected by Transport Roads and Maritime Services HVIS.
  • Drivers are authorised and accredited by Transport for NSW, with training, medical checks and strong driving history.
  • Engine Number 9 NSW PTY LTD is fully insured.
  • We carry the correct green slip for our licensed 22 seater bus.
  • We hold a current public liability policy.
  • All staff attending children’s packages must hold a Working With Children Check clearance.

Safety & Event Standards

  • Our fleet is fitted with fire extinguishers, first aid kits, bottled water and on-board surveillance safety cameras.
  • We cater for guest special needs and requests where possible.
  • Our goal is to deliver quality service, safety and a smooth event from start to finish.
  • Don’t settle for anything less.
  • Engine Number 9 NSW PTY LTD has always been an industry leader in party trucks and buses that specialise in transporting children.

The Fine Print

  • Open to ages 0 to 12 only.
  • Maximum of 20 people: 18 children and 2 adults for supervision.
  • Hirer must nominate a minimum of 2 adult parent supervisors per fire engine ride.
  • Additional frameable certificates are $1 each.
  • Pick up and drop off from single locations only.
  • Additional extras permissible.
  • Available 7 days a week.
  • Quick bite food stop on request.
  • Scheduled toilet stop on request.
  • Strictly an under 18s drug and alcohol free event.
  • Strictly no alcohol or smoking on board.
  • Engine Number 9 reserves the right to remove anyone breaking the rules.
  • Reduced passenger numbers when hiring on-board guest entertainment.
  • $250 deposit required to secure your booking.
  • Outer suburbs surcharges may apply.
  • Toll way usage is $25 per usage.
  • All terms and conditions must be agreed to.
